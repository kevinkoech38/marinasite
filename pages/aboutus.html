More about us

Events held by us

Our job is to inspire the young we offer to have schools bring their students to to learn more about boats, sailing and marine life. We show them the wonders of sea life. The marina has an old decommisioned ship that we use as our museum.

One of our more special events is the boat build off. Builders from all around come to show off their amazing work and share it with us. In this event we get to see the crazy and whacky designs around.

Boat racing is one of the more interesting ones where we have a couple of different categories, modified and stock. Under the modified category here you will find every thing from proper racing boats to hovercrafts. It is quite interesting to watch.

Our jet ski racing is one of the more famous of all our events. It is enjoyed by everyone, our current champion is our very own Nicholas.

We prioritize you

We believe in working closely with our clients and are willing to adapt our services to respond to the shifting priorities of each engagement. We above all else value your comfort and we apreciate your joining our family.
