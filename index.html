The Home for your vessel.

The Yacht and cruise marina is the perfect home for your vessel. We offer a list of services for all the vessels that are entrusted to us and we make sure that you have peace knowing your vessel is safe from all manner of tampering.

With us your vessel will be in the best of care, and we thank you for chooosing the Yacht and Cruise marina.

Our Services

Our marina offers the following list of services:

  • Boat Cleaning & Maintainance

    We offer a boat cleaning service for interior and exterior

  • Boat Service

    We have mechanics who specialize in diesel engines and can also service any other type of boat

  • Boat Recovery

    We recover stranded, sunken and crashed vessels. We have tug boats that can tug the largest of ships.

  • Boat Repair

    We repair most types of damages & restore boats. Our mechanics repair fiberglass, steel, wood & they painting as well.

Our marina Events

Our marina hosts events 4 times a year. The 2 favorites being speed boat racing and Jet ski racing.

The boat races are one of the most famous of all the events, since the races are open and even modified boats are allowed to compete.
